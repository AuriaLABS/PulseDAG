# 30-day burn-in policy

Smart contracts remain blocked until the testnet completes 30 stable days.

## Burn-in sequence
- 24h smoke
- 72h smoke
- 7-day pre-burn-in
- 30-day full burn-in

## Success criteria
- node stays healthy
- lag converges
- restarts recover cleanly
- no repeated corruption/rebuild loops
- external miner remains stable
- journal shows manageable alert rates
